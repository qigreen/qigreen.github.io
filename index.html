Biography

I am currently a Postdoc National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition, Institute of Automation Chinese Academy of Sciences and University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. My supervisors are Prof. Xiaopeng Zhang and Dr. Dong-Ming Yan. Since October 2017, I participated in an international co-supervision Ph.D. program at GIPSA-Lab, Grenoble, France, and was supervised by Prof. Denis Pellerin and Dr. Kai Wang. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree from Wuhan University of Technology in 2014.

My research interest includes image forensics, computer graphics and deep learning.
